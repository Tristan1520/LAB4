    • Characters Cooler Than Real Life

    This page includes a list of the coolest characters ever created in fiction. There is no particular order to this list, but the character on top is there for a reason. Each character has their own traits that make them unique in their own ways, but they all have one thing in common. They're cooler than any non-fictional character could ever be, as they are not defined by the restraints of the real world.
